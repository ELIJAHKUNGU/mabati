About Us BEST STEEL
TIANJIN BEST STEEL TRADE CO.,LTD is one of China leading steel pipe & fitting exporter and processing factory. Our factory located in Daqiuzhuang Town, Tianjin China which is the center of China steel pipes. BEST STEEL focus on high quality steel, and get ISO9001 audit. Here we provide pipes & fittings according to API5L, APT5CT, ASTM A53, BS 1387, JIS3461 , ASME 16.9,etc standard. We can supply stainless steel pipe, welded (ERW, SAW, SSAW) pipe, seamless (Cold draw & hot rolled ) pipe, pipe fittings with a wide range of material such a S235,S355,S460,A355P1,201,304,316,X52, A105,etc.Our processing factory can supply thread, cut, groove, drill, oil, paint, galvanized, PE anti corrosion, bevel work. Our steel pipes widely used in South America, Middle East, South Asia, Africa and other counties. For high quality and good reputation, Now many clients let us help purchase other products such as roof sheet, angle bar, flat bar, rebar, steel sheet, GI, PPGI, GL, beams, channels, wire, mesh, scaffolding, Aluminum and cooper products. Then we formed our inspection team to control each container goods is BEST STEEL.
